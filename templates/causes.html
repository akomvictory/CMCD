Our Core Objectives

  • To creatively use media as an advocacy platform to improve the health and wellbeing of different communities.
  • To use different media platforms to promote access to quality education
  • To amplify the voices of marginalized communities and enable participation in decision making and governance
  • To enable communities, tell their stories in the world.

Thematic Areas

  • Health and wellbeing: TB, malaria, mental health, drug use etc: CMCD plans to use media as a tool to reduce the prevalence of harmful practices and health related issues, impact health policy making and promote health education in communities
  • Human Development: We would use media to influence youth development within the communities we serve
  • Social justice (encompasses human rights, governance, gender, equity): We would help drive causes and interventions to ensure equality, governance, and enforcement of human rights.
  • Peace building and conflict resolution: With the use of our platforms, we would be engaging in conflict resolution and peace building in our communities.
  • 5. Good governance: We would contribute to efforts to strengthen government institutions, ensure inclusiveness in the process of policy formulation for communities through capacity building and mobilization.
