Approach/ Strategy

Media Content:Creative Media Centre for Development uses a participatory approach to produce media content, ensure individuals; groups and community are engaged in the creation of media content. We use storytelling as a powerful tool to deliver critical information about social that issues impact a community. These stories are designed to educate audience as well as influence attitudes and promote behaviour change in an engaging way.

Creative Media Centre for Development works alongside communities, providing skills and resources to create contents produced by the community, for the community. Working in close collaboration with local groups as well as humanitarian, experts and other stakeholders. we produce media contents (visual, audio, prints), and other media that resonate with audiences, address their most pressing concerns and effect powerful change.

Training:Through film, photography, and digital media education, Creative Media Centre for Development, gives youth, vulnerable groups and communities, the technical skills necessary to explore issues within their own communities and to express themselves creatively.

Community Outreach:Creative Media Centre for Development uses community outreach as an avenue to share it produce media content, while also using art such as music, poetry (Spoken words), drama, film and graffiti to inform and educate communities on issues around, public health, gender, corruption, transparency and accountability, governance and human rights.

Film Festival: :Creative Media Centre for Development uses the power of films to inspire, inform, educate and stimulating debates regarding human rights, and critical social issues through film festivals.
